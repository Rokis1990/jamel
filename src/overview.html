Jamel (Java Agent-based Macro-Economic Laboratory) is an open source, macro-economic agent-based model with heterogeneous households, heterogeneous firms and a banking sector. It provides a fully decentralized and stock-flow consistent framework for the simulation and the analysis of economic dynamics.

Jamel is currently developped by Dr. P. Seppecher (original author, U. of Paris North and U. of Nice Sophia Antipolis, France) and Dr. I. Salle (U. of Amsterdam, The Netherlands).

A demo version of Jamel, together with several simulation scenarios, can be downloaded here: http://p.seppecher.free.fr/jamel/download.php.
The source code of Jamel is available here: https://github.com/pseppecher/jamel.
